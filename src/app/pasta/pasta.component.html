Ingredients

  • 1 box of pasta (any shape, we don't discriminate)
  • Water (from the tap, not the fancy stuff)
  • Salt (enough to make the ocean jealous)
  • 1 jar of sauce (homemade? lol no)
  • Cheese (the entire bag, obviously)
  • A prayer to the pasta gods

Instructions

  1. Boil water. This will take 47 years. Do not watch it.
  2. Add salt. More. No, more than that. Perfect.
  3. Add pasta. Stir once and then forget about it for exactly too long.
  4. Realize the pasta is sticking. Panic. Stir aggressively.
  5. Drain pasta but burn yourself on the steam. Curse loudly.
  6. Dump entire jar of sauce on pasta. Mix it like you mean it.
  7. Add cheese until you can no longer see the pasta.
  8. Eat directly from the pot because dishes are for quitters.

Chef's Notes

Pairs well with: Netflix, regret, and a second serving.
Prep time: 5 minutes
Cook time: 10 minutes
Time spent convincing yourself this counts as a balanced meal: 45 minutes

* No Italian grandmothers were harmed in the making of this recipe. They were, however, deeply disappointed.
